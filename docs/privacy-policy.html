Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

CatTracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter (cat profiles, health logs, weight records) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage. No data is sent to any server or cloud service. If you delete the app, all data will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

CatTracker uses RevenueCat for in-app purchase management. RevenueCat processes only the transaction data required by Apple/Google for purchases. No health data or cat information is shared with RevenueCat or any other third party.

Children's Privacy

CatTracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is a pet health tracker and does not require any personal identification.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@dramavision.top.
